duativ is a UK based AI Automation Agency, specialized in transforming businesses through intelligent AI powered automation solutions.

History

Duativ was founded in 2025 by Marc Bruisten, a seasoned professional with extensive experience in digital environments and roles varying from web development, automation, Scrum Mastering and agile coaching for global brands like Bugaboo and Vodafone. Marc's deep understanding of business agility and technology, combined with the transformative power of AI, fuels duativ's passion for helping businesses to operate smarter, faster, and more efficiently.

Approach

duativ takes a human-centered and agile approach to AI integration, driven by the believe that AI is never a goal by itself, but a catalyst for innovation. duativ works closely with each client to understand their unique challenges and develop tailored solutions that drive real business value, with a focus on simple, fast, practical and future proof solutions that deliver measurable results.

Mission

To empower small and medium businesses to harness AI-driven automation, freeing them from repetitive tasks and unlocking new opportunities for growth. duativ is founded on the believe that AI isn't replacing humans; it's allowing them to do more, think bigger, and innovate smarter. By blending agile expertise with cutting-edge technology, we create intelligent solutions that streamline operations, enhance decision-making, and help businesses stay ahead of the curve.
