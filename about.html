duativ is a UK based AI Automation Agency, specialized in transforming businesses through intelligent AI powered automation solutions.

Our History

Duativ was founded in 2025 by Marc Bruisten, a seasoned professional with extensive experience in digital environments and roles varying from web development, automation and agile coaching for global brands like Bugaboo and Vodafone. Marc's deep understanding of business agility and technology, combined with the transformative power of AI, fuels our passion for helping businesses to operate smarter, faster, and more efficiently.

Our Approach

We take a human-centered approach to AI integration, we believe that AI is never a goal by itself, but a catalyst for innovation. We work closely with each client to understand their unique challenges and develop tailored solutions that drive real business value. We focus on practical, implementable solutions that deliver measurable results.

Our Mission

To empower small and medium businesses to harness AI-driven automation, freeing them from repetitive tasks and unlocking new opportunities for growth. We don't believe that AI is replacing humans; it's empowering them to do more, think bigger, and innovate smarter. By blending expertise with cutting-edge technology, we create intelligent solutions that streamline operations, enhance decision-making, and help businesses stay ahead of the curve.
